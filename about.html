Our Story
The Beginning

Your gateway to exquisite fragrances

Welcome to Ssniff N Smell — where we believe that a fragrance is more than just a scent. It's a journey, a memory, and an experience.

We proudly present a curated range of handcrafted niche fragrance dupes, carefully created to transport you across moments, emotions, and destinations with every wear.

Our collection is designed to suit every personality and every occasion. From timeless designer inspirations to rare niche creations, each fragrance is chosen for its uniqueness and quality. Our premium oils are crafted with high-grade ingredients to ensure long-lasting, captivating aromas that stay with you throughout the day.

We are based in Nagpur and Kolkata — and we ship across all of India, because great fragrance belongs everywhere.

"Fragrance is the only art that can take you back in time through your senses. At Ssniff N Smell, we understand the emotional connection between scent and memory."
