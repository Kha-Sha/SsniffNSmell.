Why Sample First

The right way to buy fragrance

A fragrance on paper, on someone else's skin, on a strip — is a completely different fragrance than the one on yours. Your skin chemistry, your body heat, your diet all change how a scent develops.

That's why we offer every fragrance as a 2ml sample — because ₹99-₹149 is a much better way to spend before ₹2,000.

Two millilitres is enough for 4-6 full wears. Enough to know if it belongs in your rotation. Enough to know if it's the one.
